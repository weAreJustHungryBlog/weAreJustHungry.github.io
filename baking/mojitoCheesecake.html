Mojito Cheesecake


Living in Spain implies getting through very hot summer days. What better way of doing this than to combine one of our favourite drinks with cheesecake. This Mojito Cheesecake Recipe is a perfect dessert for a hot summer day or even for a great sweet snack to share with friends.
The following recipe has been adjusted from a recipe that we had found online. We increased the amounts of cheesecake base in order for the cake to be bigger.

  • 200 gr ginger biscuits
  • 50 gr butter, melted
  • 400 gr cream cheese
  • 1 tin condensed milk
  • 150 ml double cream
  • zest of 3 limes
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 5 tablespoons rum
  • Handful chopped mint leaves
  • Raspberries and sliced lime for decoration

  1. Place biscuits in a plastic bag and crush until fine.
  2. Combine them with the melted butter.
  3. Press the mixture into the bottom of a cake tin and refrigerate for at least 30 mins.
  4. To make the filling, beat together the cream cheese and the condensed milk.
  5. In a separate bowl, whip the cream until it starts to stiffen, then add them cream cheese mix to it.
  6. Stir in the lime zest, vanilla, rum, and chopped mint.
  7. Pour the mix into the tin on top of the biscuit base and refrigerate for at least two hours until it is set.
  8. Decorate using limes, raspberries and mint leaves.

The cake is best served chilled. Different further variations could be considered. For example, different biscuits such as almond biscuits could be used for the base. The raspberries that were used as decoration could also be mixed into the cheesecake mixture to give it a fruitier taste.
